The Art of Chiquiat

Chiquiat currently resides in Germany and enjoys her free time painting and travelling with the family. She uses watercolor and acrylic paints for her artworks, which are usually inspired by the places she has already visited. She loves to use mixed media such as paper mâché for her canvass and sometimes highlights her Artworks with glitters.
