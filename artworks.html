Die Insel (Acrylic on Canvas, 50 x 30 cm)


Die Insel or the Island belongs to the 3-part painting inspired by the charming town of Wasserburg am Inn. This was the last one that was painted to complete the series. The incomplete bridge as seen on the right bottom is connected to the other painting, "die Brücke".
